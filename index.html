January brings us firefox 85

To wrap up january, we are proud to bring you the release of firefox 85. In this version we are bringin you support for the :focus-visible pseudo-class in CSS and associated devtools, and the complete removal of flash suppport from Firefox.

news coverage and some surprises

The release of Apple Silicon-based Macs at the end of last year generated a flurry of news coverage and some surprises at the manchine's performance. This post details some background information on the experience of porting Firefox to run natively on these CPUs.

We'll start with some background on the mac transition and give an overvierw of firefox internals that needed to know about the new architecture, before movving on the concept of universal binaries

We'll the explain how DRM/EMEe works on the new platform, talk about our experience with macOS big sur, and discuss various upadter problemas we had to deal with We'll conclude with the release and an overview of various other improvenents that are in the pipeline
